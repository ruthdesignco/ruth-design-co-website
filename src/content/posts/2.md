Great delivery starts with great intake. Here’s how we compress client onboarding into a single day without losing quality.

## The four artifacts
1. **Intent doc** — goals, audience, and primary CTA in 10 questions.
2. **Offer clarity** — three paths only: Launch, Improve, or Maintain.
3. **Asset drop** — logo, palette, copy bank, testimonials, product shots.
4. **Success criteria** — one metric we will move (demos, trials, or calls).

## What happens in 24 hours
- **Hour 1–4:** Client completes the intent doc and uploads assets.
- **Hour 5–6:** We turn goals into a project brief and confirm scope.
- **Hour 7–24:** Backlog is created with atomic tasks and owners; a read-only dashboard goes live.

### Communication rules
- Async-first: loom updates and written feedback over meetings.
- Two windows for questions: 10am and 4pm local time.
- Changes outside scope become add-ons, not surprises.

Onboarding that is fast, predictable, and respectful of everyone’s time sets the tone for the entire engagement.
