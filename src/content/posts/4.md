Your clients don’t want vanity numbers; they want proof the engagement worked. These are the metrics we track on every productized design sprint.

## Leading indicators
- **Time to first draft:** under 48 hours from kickoff.
- **Review cycle count:** aim for ≤2 blocking rounds.
- **Component reuse:** percentage of UI built from the design system.

## Lagging indicators
- **Conversion lift:** primary CTA clicks or form starts after launch.
- **Bounce rate delta:** homepage and pricing page post-launch.
- **Support tickets:** reduction in “how do I…?” requests tied to UI clarity.

## How we report
- One-page PDF: goal, changes shipped, metrics, next actions.
- Screenshots of before/after for immediate visual proof.
- A 15-minute readout call with a single yes/no: “Did we move the metric we agreed on?”

Keep the measurement tight, transparent, and tied to the promise of the productized offer.
