Pricing is where productized services live or die. Here’s the ladder we use to keep decisions fast and margins safe.

## Three rungs, no custom quotes

1. **Launch** — one-page site or small feature, 3-day turnaround, fixed revisions.
2. **Improve** — conversion-focused refresh, analytics + A/B scaffolding, 2-week window.
3. **Maintain** — monthly design/dev queue with a capped number of requests.

## Guardrails that protect margin

- Clear scope bullets and a “not included” list in every offer.
- Timeboxed QA: one blocking, one polish pass.
- Add-ons priced separately (extra pages, animations, integrations).

## Communicating price

- Anchor with outcomes, not hours.
- Publish the pricing grid publicly—confidence builds trust.
- Include a “why this costs what it costs” paragraph for procurement.

Consistency in pricing creates consistency in delivery—and happier clients.
