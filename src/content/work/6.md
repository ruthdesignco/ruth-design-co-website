# Procreate: elevating the digital canvas

Procreate wanted to spotlight its creative power without overwhelming new artists. We refined onboarding, simplified tool discovery, and tuned performance for complex canvases.

## Client goals

1. Reduce new-user friction and help artists create a first piece quickly.
2. Make advanced brushes and layers discoverable without clutter.
3. Improve perceived performance on large canvases and older devices.
4. Deliver a brand refresh that feels premium but approachable.

## What we delivered

1. **Guided first canvas**  
   A friendly walkthrough that introduces brushes, layers, and gestures in a single guided flow.
2. **Curated brush sets**  
   Collections organized by style with previews and quick-apply actions to encourage experimentation.
3. **Performance tuning**  
   Render pipeline tweaks and smarter caching to keep interactions smooth on heavy files.
4. **Brand and UI polish**  
   Updated iconography, motion, and microcopy to keep the experience cohesive across iPad and iPhone.

## Results

1. **Faster activation**: more users completed the first drawing tutorial and saved a canvas.
2. **Deeper feature use**: curated brush sets increased adoption of advanced tools.
3. **Smoother sessions**: performance improvements reduced lag reports on large canvases.

Procreate now greets artists with a smoother, more inspiring first experience while keeping pros productive.
